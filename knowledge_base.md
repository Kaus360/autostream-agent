# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Pricing Plans
- Basic Plan: $29/month — 10 videos/month, 720p resolution
- Pro Plan: $79/month — Unlimited videos, 4K resolution, AI captions

## Features
- Automated video editing powered by AI
- Smart scene detection and trimming
- Auto-generated captions and subtitles (Pro only)
- Background music suggestions
- One-click export to YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram
- Cloud storage for all projects

## Company Policies
- No refunds after 7 days of purchase
- Free trial available for 14 days (no credit card required)
- 24/7 support available only on Pro plan
- Basic plan users have access to email support (72-hour response time)

## Supported Platforms
- YouTube
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- Facebook
- Twitter/X

## Technical Requirements
- Internet connection required
- Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux via browser
- Mobile app available on iOS and Android (Pro plan only)

## Getting Started
1. Sign up at autostream.io
2. Choose your plan
3. Upload your raw video footage
4. Let AutoStream's AI edit and enhance your video
5. Review, tweak, and publish directly from the dashboard
